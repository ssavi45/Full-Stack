How do keep balance between your work and life?

Well it's not that easy, but you can try to do it by following some rules. Like,


Wake up early in the morning
Eat healthy food
Exercise regularly
Spend time with family and friends
Take breaks during work
Set boundaries between work and personal life

What can you to keep your mind fresh for work?

Well, you can take some days off, and plan a vacation with your friends and family
